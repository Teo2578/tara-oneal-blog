Growing up Growing up in the city of Portland Maine, I was introduced to a unique, cultural city that inspired my childhood. I took advantage of trying new activities from sports, hobbies to talents of all kinds. By the age of seven, I found myself on two travel sport teams and an active upcoming artist, praciting my abilites in various ways such as watercoloring and crafting. As a growing and talented indivdial, I had develped strong social skills, getting involved in non profit organizaitons, volunteer work and being a strong advocate for various organization with my father in my community. All of these abilities, led to creating myself. I have various talents in athletics, an eye for design and strong social abilities.

Fashion At a young age, I developed a strong sense and nose for style and design of all types. I became very obsessed with boho-chic style, also known as an idie style. From this, I decided to express my love for fashion and trends. I hope you'll enjoy this website I put together!
