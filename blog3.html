Her outfit is georgous. What's a better combination than a brick red with an ivory tiny and a lush shine? Stephany is a beautiful model for many of my porflio works and fashion imagery.

Rompers are the way to go when it comes to the soft warm summer breeze. These are a cute boho inlfuenced clothing piece that has become a huge trend throughout the past few years in the United States.

What are the fashion rules of thumb with rompers? NOTHING! They are a free length and comportable size around the hips and thighs. Many way rompers with cute long sandals that wrap around the leg, flip flips, if it's a fancy romper (a.k.a lace and silk!) definitely a wedge or small pump. Only rule of thumn, Never wear tights! Some try to pull this off but it isn't a thing. Rompers can be offered in various fabrics such as mostly cotton although there's polyester, silk, mesh and even a jean fabric! Rombers can be warn with sweaters, summer cardigans, light shalls and even really cute sweatshirts.

Boho/ Boho Chic is a beautiful fashion hippie style that is influenced by 70’s fabric and became a drastic fashion throughout the 20’s. Boho chick is based on “boho” homeless and “chic” stylish hippy elegant french influenced style.
